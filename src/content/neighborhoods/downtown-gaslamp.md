## Overview

Downtown San Diego offers the ultimate urban lifestyle with high-rise living, world-class restaurants, and easy access to everything the city has to offer. The historic Gaslamp Quarter provides entertainment and dining, while surrounding neighborhoods offer distinct personalities.

**Location:** Heart of San Diego along the bay, encompassing Gaslamp Quarter, East Village, Little Italy, Marina District, and Columbia.

## Real Estate Market

Downtown real estate is dominated by condominiums ranging from studio apartments to luxury penthouses with panoramic bay views. New developments continue to add inventory.

### Neighborhoods Within Downtown

- **Gaslamp Quarter** - Historic heart with restaurants and nightlife
- **Little Italy** - Charming neighborhood with excellent Italian dining
- **East Village** - Near Petco Park, rapidly developing
- **Marina District** - Waterfront high-rises with bay views
- **Columbia** - Mix of residential and commercial

## Why Buy Downtown

1. **Walkability** - No car needed for daily life
2. **Entertainment** - Restaurants, bars, sports, and culture
3. **Waterfront** - Easy access to bay and harbor
4. **Convenience** - Close to airport, train, and transit
5. **Investment** - Strong rental demand from young professionals
