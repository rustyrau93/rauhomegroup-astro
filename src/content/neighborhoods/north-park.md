## Overview

North Park is San Diego's vibrant urban heartbeat, combining historic charm with modern cool. This walkable neighborhood is known for its thriving craft beer scene, independent restaurants, and eclectic mix of vintage shops and contemporary boutiques.

**Location:** Central San Diego, just east of Hillcrest and north of downtown, bordering Balboa Park.

**What Makes It Unique:** North Park has transformed from a quiet residential area to one of San Diego's most dynamic neighborhoods while maintaining its historic character. The community strongly supports local businesses and artists.

## Real Estate Market

North Park offers a mix of charming historic homes and modern developments. Craftsman bungalows and Spanish Revival homes are highly sought after, while new condo developments offer urban living options.

### Why Buy in North Park

1. **Walkability** - One of San Diego's most walkable neighborhoods
2. **Culture** - Thriving arts, food, and craft beer scene
3. **Location** - Minutes from downtown, Balboa Park, and beaches
4. **Character** - Historic architecture with modern amenities
5. **Appreciation** - Strong value growth as urban living gains popularity
