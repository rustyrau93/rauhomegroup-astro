## Overview

La Jolla (Spanish for "The Jewel") is San Diego's crown jewel of coastal luxury living. Perched on dramatic cliffs overlooking the Pacific Ocean, this world-renowned community offers an unparalleled combination of natural beauty, intellectual culture, and sophisticated urban amenities.

**Location:** Northwest San Diego, approximately 12 miles from downtown San Diego, nestled between Pacific Beach to the south and Torrey Pines to the north.

**What Makes It Unique:** La Jolla seamlessly blends breathtaking natural landscapes with world-class institutions like UC San Diego, Scripps Institution of Oceanography, and the Salk Institute.

## Real Estate Market

La Jolla offers diverse price points from entry-level condos around $800K to ultra-luxury oceanfront estates exceeding $20M. Most single-family homes range from $1.8M to $8.5M depending on location and views.

### Sub-Neighborhoods

- **La Jolla Farms** - Ultra-luxury estates with sweeping ocean views (Median: $8.5M)
- **La Jolla Shores** - Beachfront living with direct sand access (Median: $3.2M)
- **The Village** - Walkable luxury lifestyle with shops and restaurants (Median: $2.4M)
- **Windansea Beach** - Famous surf break and dramatic coastline (Median: $3.8M)
- **Bird Rock** - Entry-level La Jolla with strong community feel (Median: $1.8M)

## Lifestyle & Culture

La Jolla exudes refined sophistication with a relaxed coastal sensibility. It's where Nobel Prize winners share coffee shops with surfers, and world-class scientists live next door to tech entrepreneurs.

### Annual Events
- La Jolla Art & Wine Festival
- La Jolla Open Aire Market (Sundays)
- La Jolla Concours d'Elegance
- Christmas by the Sea
