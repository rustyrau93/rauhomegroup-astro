## Overview

Carmel Valley is San Diego's quintessential family neighborhood, known for its excellent schools, safe streets, and abundant amenities. This master-planned community offers the perfect balance of suburban comfort and coastal accessibility.

## Real Estate Market

Carmel Valley offers a range of housing options from condos and townhomes to luxury single-family estates. The area continues to see strong demand from families seeking top schools and safe communities.

### Popular Sub-Areas

- **Pacific Highlands Ranch** - Newer development with modern homes
- **Torrey Hills** - Upscale homes with canyon views
- **One Paseo Area** - Walkable to shopping and dining
- **Carmel Country Highlands** - Larger lots and established homes

## Schools

Carmel Valley is served by the award-winning Del Mar Union and San Dieguito Union school districts. Canyon Crest Academy is consistently ranked among California's best public high schools.
