## Overview

Del Mar is a charming coastal city known for its "Where the Turf Meets the Surf" motto, combining world-class horse racing with pristine beaches. This small but affluent community offers a perfect blend of beach town charm and sophisticated living.

**Location:** Just north of La Jolla along the coast, approximately 20 miles north of downtown San Diego.

**What Makes It Unique:** Del Mar offers an intimate small-town feel with big-city amenities. The iconic Del Mar Racetrack brings seasonal excitement, while the village's boutiques and restaurants provide year-round charm.

## Real Estate Market

Del Mar's limited land area and strict building codes keep inventory low and values high. Most properties offer ocean views or are within walking distance to the beach.

### Neighborhoods

- **Del Mar Beach Colony** - Beachfront homes steps from the sand
- **Del Mar Heights** - Larger lots with panoramic ocean views
- **The Village** - Walkable downtown with shops and restaurants
- **Olde Del Mar** - Historic cottages with old-world charm

## Schools

Del Mar is served by the Del Mar Union School District, consistently rated among the best in California. Torrey Pines High School is one of the top-ranked public high schools in the nation.
